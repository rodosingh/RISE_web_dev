Thrust Areas of Incubation

Affordable Health-Care

  • Targeted Drug Delivery by Nanocarriers: From Polymers to Peptide Nanomaterials.
  • Affordable use of Probiotics against diarrheal illness in children.
  • Inexpensive point-of-care diagnostics using a microfluidics chip.
  • Affordable health care diagnostic- integrating high throughput sequencing for disease detection.

Smart Materials for Renewable Energy and Nanotechnology

  • Large area, highly efficient and stable perovskite solar cells.
  • Splitting of sea water into hydrogen fuel.
  • Microbial and chemical conversion of biomass into designer diesel.
  • CO2 reduction, insertion reactions and possible applications in environmental remediation.
  • Integrated nano-photonics initiative: spectroscopy, devices, sensing, and patterning.

Big Data Analytics

  • Statistical Modeling and Bayesian Inference.
  • Network Analysis of large scale data.
  • Wavelet analysis using real world data.
